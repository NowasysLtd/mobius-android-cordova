The Hotel

Hotel Laval

The La Val Hotel is lovated 1300 metres above sea level on the sunny plateau of Breil/Brigels. It offers spacious rooms with south-facing balconies, an award-winning 500 m2 spa area, and free parking. The spa area offers a relaxation oasis, massages, kosmetics treatments, saunas, steam baths and a heated wellness pool. All rooms in the Hotel La Val Bergspa Brigels feature a bathroom, a flat-screen TV and a safe. Some a Nespresso coffee machine. Swiss, international and Mediterranean cuisine is served in the the Bistro and in the restaurent. There is also a separate Smokers Lounge and a walk-in wine closet. Old wooden ski and sleds add to the Alpine charm of the hotel. The La Val hotel is the ideal base for trips over the Lukmanierpass to Ticino or over the Oberalp pass to Central Switzerland. The Burleun ski lift is 1 km away. Hiking, Nordic walking, cycling, mini golf, tennis, climbing, golf and river rafting are popular activities in the region. Winter activities include skiing, snowboarding, sledding, ice skating and snow-shoeing. Guests receive a 20% discount on the local golf course's green fees.
